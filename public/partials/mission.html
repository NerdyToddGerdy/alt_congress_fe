Mission and Vision

  • Vision

    To foster a more open-minded and collaborative electorate willing to let go of ideological theory and debate facts in the search for pragmatic legislation.

  • Mission

    Facilitate the creation of non-partisan and pragmatic legislation for the people and by the people.

  • Impact

    An engaged and knowledgeable US electorate that prospers with greater opportunities for all.

  • Values

    Collaboration, Equity, Community, Respect, Citizenship, Reason, Character, Innovation
